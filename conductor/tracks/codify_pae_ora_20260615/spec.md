# Specification: Codify Pae Ora Act 2022

## Purpose
Codify primary care funding integration principles and parameters under the Pae Ora (Healthy Futures) Act 2022 to support health system simulation modeling.

## Requirements
- Introduce health funding status variables under `openfisca_aotearoa/variables/acts/pae_ora/`.
- Provide parameters mapping primary health organization (PHO) funding caps.
- Add citation links to the official Pae Ora legislation page.
