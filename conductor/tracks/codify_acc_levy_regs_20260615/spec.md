# Specification: Codify ACC Earners' Levy Regulations

## Purpose
Codify Earners' Levy rates, maximum caps, and regulations under the Accident Compensation (Earners' Levy) Regulations.

## Requirements
- Introduce ACC Earner's Levy parameters and rates under `openfisca_aotearoa/parameters/acc/earners_levy.yaml`.
- Provide calculation logic under `openfisca_aotearoa/variables/acts/acc/earners_levy.py` to calculate earners' levy.
- Add citation links to the official Accident Compensation Regulations page.
