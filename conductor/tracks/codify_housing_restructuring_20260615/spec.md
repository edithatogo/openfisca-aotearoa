# Specification: Codify Housing Restructuring Act 1992

## Purpose
Codify social housing income-related rent calculators governed under the Housing Restructuring and Tenancy Matters Act 1992.

## Requirements
- Introduce social housing eligibility variables under `openfisca_aotearoa/variables/acts/housing_restructuring/`.
- Provide variables calculating income-related rent based on assessable income.
- Add citation links to the official Housing Restructuring and Tenancy Matters Act page.
